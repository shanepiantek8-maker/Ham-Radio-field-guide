▸ Field Expedient SWR Check
▸ No VNA / SWR Meter? Use Your Radio
1. Set radio to lowest power (5W or less).
2. Key a steady carrier:
  · CW mode — hold key down
  · FM mode — key PTT (full carrier)
  · RTTY/Digital mode — emit tone
  · SSB — whistle steady tone into mic
3. Watch built-in SWR/PWR meter if available.
4. High SWR = many radios automatically fold back power — reflected power showing means mismatch.
5. Trim wire slightly, retest. Wire too short = can't fix. Always cut long, trim back.

⚠ Carrier emission mode varies by radio model.
IC-7200: RTTY mode emits steady tone on PTT. FT-series: CW or FM. Check your radio manual for carrier/tone emission method before field deployment.

▸ End Termination — Crimp Loop Method
Recommended: Terminate each wire end in a crimped loop (loop + crimp sleeve) attached to your end insulator/cordage. The crimp loop is the true electrical end of the radiator — clean, fixed, repeatable.

No fold-back tuning loops needed. If trimming, cut equally from both ends at the crimp, re-crimp a new loop, and re-measure. This keeps the leg length and the trim calculator above in agreement — no hidden "extra" length from fold-backs to account for.
